Welcome to the website of the Unmanned Systems and Intelligent Control (USIC) Group at Beijing Institute of Technology. We are a research group focusing on the development of intelligent systems for unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and other robotic platforms.

Our research interests lie in the intersection of intelligent methods and unmanned systems, with a focus on the following areas:

  • System design and integration for autonomous unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and other robotic platforms,
  • Intelligent methods for perception, planning, and control,
  • Multi-robot systems and swarm intelligence, coordination, and collaboration,
  • Learning methods for robust and efficient operation in real-world environments.
